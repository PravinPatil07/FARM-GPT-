Bone Meal Fertilizer

Description:
Bone Meal provides phosphorus and calcium to plants,along with a largely inconsequential amount of nitrogen the N-P-K rating of bone meal is typically 3-15-0. it increases phosphorus in soil for optimal spring gardening result



Content:
1. Phosphorus (P): 15-27%



- Points: - Phosphorus (P) is essential for root development, bulb initiation, and overall plant vigor in garlic.
- Provides a slow-release source of phosphorus, ensuring continuous availability to garlic plants during their growth cycle.
- Helps promote strong root systems and enhances bulb formation, leading to larger and healthier garlic bulbs.
- Best applied at planting or as a side dressing during the early stages of garlic growth to support root development and bulb formation.
