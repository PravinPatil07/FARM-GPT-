Triple Superphosphate

Rs 1350 per Bag

50 kg Bag

Description:
Triple Superphosphate is a phosphatic fertilizer used to supply phosphorus, an essential nutrient for rice plants. It stimulates root development, aids in flower and seed formation, and promotes early crop establishment



- Content:
1. Nitrogen (N): 0%
2. Phosphorus (P): 46%
3. Potassium (K): 0%



- Points:
- High phosphorus content is crucial for root growth and flowering.
- Ideal for application during the early stages of rice growth.
- Slow-release phosphorus source, providing long-term nutrient availability to rice plants.
