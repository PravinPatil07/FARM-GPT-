Calcium Nitrate

Rs 140 per Kg

50 kg Bag

Description:
Calcium Nitrate used as a basal application and as foliar spray also. Useful for all crops. is a water soluble formulation that provides fast acting nitrogen along with calcium to increase food quantity.



Content:
1. Calcium (Ca): 15.5%
2. Nitrogen (N) : 19%



- Points:
- Calcium helps prevent blossom end rot, a common disorder in tomatoes caused by calcium deficiency.
- Nitrogen promotes vegetative growth and helps maintain plant health.
- Best applied as a side dressing during the fruiting stage to prevent calcium deficiencies and promote healthy fruit development.
- Particularly beneficial in regions with acidic soils or where calcium deficiency is common.
